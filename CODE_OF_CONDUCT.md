# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and collaborative community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate in discussions and reviews.
* Providing constructive feedback and accepting feedback gracefully.
* Being welcoming to new contributors and helping them get started.
* Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
* Taking responsibility for mistakes, apologizing when appropriate, and learning from them.
* Focusing on what is best for the project and its community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language of any kind.
* Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
* Trolling, deliberate intimidation, or disruptive behavior.
* Publishing or sharing another person's private information without permission.
* Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or community setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, discussions, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including issuing warnings, temporarily restricting participation, or permanently banning contributors from the project.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

* GitHub issues
* Pull requests
* Discussions
* Project documentation
* Any other official communication channels related to this project

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainer by opening a private security advisory or contacting the maintainer through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled respectfully and, where possible, confidentially.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will evaluate reported incidents fairly and consistently, taking into account the context and impact of the behavior. Responses may include:

1. A private warning.
2. A temporary suspension from participating in the project.
3. A permanent ban from the project for severe or repeated violations.
